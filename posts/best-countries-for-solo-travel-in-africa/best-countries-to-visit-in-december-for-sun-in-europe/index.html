Best Countries to Visit in December for Sun in Europe#

When the winter blues hit, and the days grow shorter, a dose of sunshine can do wonders for your spirits. If you’re looking for a warm escape in December, Europe has plenty of sunny destinations to choose from.

Here are some of the best countries to visit in December for sun in Europe:

Cyprus#

  • Cyprus is a beautiful island in the Mediterranean Sea with a mild climate year-round.
  • In December, the average temperature in Cyprus is around 65 degrees Fahrenheit, making it ideal for sunbathing, swimming, and exploring the island’s many historical and cultural attractions.

Crete#

  • Crete is the largest island in Greece and a popular tourist destination for its stunning beaches, ancient ruins, and delicious cuisine.
  • In December, the weather in Crete is still mild, with average temperatures in the mid-50s Fahrenheit.
  • This makes it a great time to visit if you want to enjoy the island’s many outdoor activities without the crowds of summer.

Canary Islands#

  • The Canary Islands are a group of islands off the coast of Africa that are part of Spain.
  • The islands have a subtropical climate, with average temperatures in December in the mid-70s Fahrenheit.
  • The Canary Islands are a great place to relax on the beach, go hiking, or explore the islands’ many natural attractions.

Portugal#

  • Portugal is a beautiful country in southwestern Europe with a mild climate and stunning coastline.
  • In December, the weather in Portugal is mild, with average temperatures in the mid-50s Fahrenheit.
  • This makes it a great time to visit if you want to explore the country’s many historical and cultural attractions without the crowds of summer.

Spain#

  • Spain is a diverse country with a rich history and culture.
  • The country has a Mediterranean climate, with average temperatures in December in the mid-50s Fahrenheit.
  • Spain is a great place to visit in December if you want to explore the country’s many historical and cultural attractions, as well as enjoy the country’s many outdoor activities.

No matter what your interests are, you’re sure to find a sunny destination in Europe that’s perfect for your December getaway. So pack your bags and get ready for some winter sun!

Tips for planning a trip to Europe in December#

A European sojourn during the holiday season in December presents a magical charm like no other. Yet, planning a trip to Europe in December requires careful consideration of factors such as weather conditions, popular destinations, and festive events.

Top 3 European Destinations#

  1. Southern Spain (Costa del Sol): 20°C (68°F)

Strolling along sun-kissed beaches, indulging in the vibrant nightlife, and exploring historic cities like Malaga under the winter sun makes Costa del Sol an ideal choice.

  1. Cyprus: 18°C (65°F)

Cyprus is a hidden gem for sun-seekers in December. Cyprus boasts a rich cultural heritage with stunning beaches, ancient ruins, and delicious Mediterranean cuisine.

  1. Canary Islands, Spain (Tenerife): 22°C (72°F)

Tenerife is famous for its lush landscapes, volcanic scenery, and beautiful beaches. The island also offers opportunities for hiking, wildlife watching, and stargazing.

Additional Tips#

  • Research local holiday traditions: Europe is renowned for its Christmas markets, festive decorations, and special events. Make time to experience these unique traditions.
  • Pack essentials for cold weather: Although you’re chasing the sun, scarves, gloves, and a warm coat are still essential.
  • Plan your itinerary carefully: Create a well-structured itinerary that allows ample time to visit key attractions while leaving room for spontaneity.

Essential packing list for a winter trip to Europe#

Gingerbread villages, twinkling lights, and mulled wine - Europe in winter is an enchanting wonderland. But as you dream of strolling the snow-covered cobblestone streets, don’t forget that packing smart is essential for a successful trip. Here’s your ultimate packing checklist:

1. Warm Clothing:#

Imagine yourself snuggled up in a cozy café, sipping on a steaming cup of hot chocolate while snowflakes dance outside. To make this dream a reality, pack:

  • Warm Coats: A water-resistant, insulated coat that reaches below the knees is a must-have. Opt for a combination of down and synthetic insulation for maximum warmth.

  • Sweaters and Knits: Layer up with thick sweaters, wool turtlenecks, and cozy knits. Merino wool is a great choice as it’s warm, breathable, and naturally odor-resistant.

  • Thermal Underwear: These are your secret weapon against the cold. Wear them under your clothes for an extra layer of warmth.

  • Scarves, Hats, and Gloves: Protect your head, neck, and hands from the chilly air. Pack a few different options to match your outfits.

2. Footwear:#

Navigating icy streets and snowy pathways requires the right footwear:

  • Winter Boots: Invest in a pair of waterproof, insulated boots with good traction. Look for boots with a sturdy sole and ankle support.

  • Sneakers: Pack a comfortable pair of sneakers or hiking boots for exploring cities and indoor attractions.

  • Wool Socks: Keep your feet cozy and warm with wool or thermal socks. Pack extra pairs for long days of sightseeing.

3. Accessories:#

Don’t forget these essential accessories:

  • Sunglasses: Even in winter, the sun can be bright, especially when reflecting off the snow. Pack a pair of UV-protective sunglasses.

  • Portable Charger: Keep your devices powered up with a portable charger. Winter weather can drain your battery faster.

  • Universal Adapter: If you’re traveling across multiple countries, pack a universal adapter to keep your devices charged.

  • Hand Sanitizer: Stay germ-free with a small bottle of hand sanitizer. It’s especially useful when you’re visiting crowded attractions or using public transportation.

4. Toiletries and Medication:#

Pack your essential toiletries and don’t forget:

  • Moisturizer: The cold, dry air can be harsh on your skin. Pack a rich, nourishing moisturizer to keep your skin hydrated.

  • Lip Balm: Protect your lips from the cold and wind with a moisturizing lip balm.

  • Hand Cream: Keep your hands soft and prevent dryness with a good hand cream.

  • Essential Medications: If you take any prescription medications, be sure to pack enough for your entire trip. Also, bring a copy of your prescriptions in case you need to refill them.

5. Travel Documents and Currency:#

Make sure you have:

  • Passport and Visa: Ensure your passport is valid for the duration of your trip. If you need a visa, apply for it well in advance.

  • Travel Insurance: Protect yourself against unexpected events with travel insurance.

  • Currency: Research the local currency and exchange some money before your trip. Consider using a travel credit card for added convenience and security.

Key Takeaways:

  • Pack warm clothing, including a water-resistant coat, sweaters, thermals, and accessories like hats, scarves, and gloves.
  • Choose comfortable, sturdy footwear, such as winter boots and sneakers.
  • Bring essential toiletries and medications, including a moisturizer, lip balm, hand cream, and any prescription medications you may need.
  • Don’t forget your travel documents, currency, and a portable charger.
  • Research the local weather and pack accordingly.

The Canary Islands: Perpetual Sunshine Awaits#

The Canary Islands, an archipelago off the coast of Africa, boasts balmy temperatures year-round. Discover the volcanic landscapes of Tenerife, the vibrant nightlife of Gran Canaria, and the serene beaches of Lanzarote. With ample sunshine and minimal rainfall, the Canary Islands are a haven for sun-seekers.

Insider Tip: Explore the stunning volcanic scenery by embarking on a hike along the trails of Mount Teide, Spain’s highest peak.

Cyprus: A Cultural Tapestry Under Azure Skies#

Cyprus, a Mediterranean gem, offers a harmonious blend of history and natural beauty. Indulge in the sun-kissed beaches of Limassol, marvel at the ancient ruins of Paphos, and bask in the vibrant culture of Nicosia. December in Cyprus promises mild temperatures, making it an ideal escape from the winter blues.

Insider Tip: Immerse yourself in the rich history of Cyprus by visiting the iconic archaeological sites of Kourion and Choirokoitia.

Malta: A Sunny Haven Steeped in History#

Malta, a small island nation south of Sicily, is a treasure trove of historical wonders and sun-soaked vistas. Explore the stunning capital city of Valletta, with its narrow streets and Baroque architecture. Discover the underground wonders of the Hypogeum of Hal Saflieni and unwind on the golden sands of Mellieha Bay.

Insider Tip: Take a day trip to the neighboring island of Gozo, renowned for its pristine beaches and tranquil atmosphere.

Portugal: Sun-Kissed Shores and Coastal Charm#

Portugal’s southern coast, particularly the Algarve region, is known for its stunning beaches, rugged cliffs, and year-round sunshine. Soak up the sun on the golden sands of Praia da Marinha, explore the picturesque towns of Lagos and Faro, and savor the country’s delectable seafood.

Insider Tip: Don’t miss the opportunity to indulge in Portugal’s culinary delights, such as the famed Pastel de Nata, a custard-filled pastry.

Greece: Sun-Drenched Islands and Ancient Wonders#

Greece, with its countless sun-kissed islands and ancient ruins, is a timeless destination. Discover the iconic Acropolis in Athens, marvel at the whitewashed buildings of Santorini, and soak up the sun on the beautiful beaches of Crete. December in Greece offers pleasant temperatures, making it an ideal time to explore its many wonders.

Insider Tip: Take a ferry to one of the lesser-known Greek islands, such as Milos or Naxos, for a tranquil escape.
