Best Countries in Europe for December Festivities

Europe in December transforms into a winter wonderland, adorned with twinkling lights, festive decorations, and the magic of the holiday season. From enchanting Christmas markets to awe-inspiring winter landscapes, Europe offers a captivating experience for travelers seeking festive cheer and unique cultural experiences. Join me as I unveil the best countries in Europe to visit in December, where you’ll be immersed in the spirit of the season and create memories that will last a lifetime.

Best Countries in Europe for Festive Charm

  • Austria: Experience the timeless charm of Vienna’s Christmas markets, where you can sip mulled wine, savor delectable treats, and admire handcrafted ornaments. Don’t miss the opportunity to ice skate in front of the stunning Rathaus (City Hall) or take a romantic horse-drawn carriage ride through the snow-covered streets.

  • Germany: Immerse yourself in the festive atmosphere of Germany’s Christmas markets, renowned for their delightful gingerbread, traditional handicrafts, and heartwarming atmosphere. Visit Nuremberg, Dresden, or Berlin to experience some of the most famous Christmas markets in the world. Take a stroll through the decorated streets, indulge in local delicacies, and be enchanted by the festive spirit.

  • France: Discover the magic of Paris during the holiday season, where the Champs-Élysées sparkles with festive lights and the Eiffel Tower stands tall, adorned with twinkling stars. Enjoy ice skating at the Grand Palais, marvel at the elegant window displays along the Avenue Montaigne, and indulge in delicious Christmas treats like bûche de Noël (Yule log cake) and vin chaud (mulled wine).

Best Countries in Europe for Winter Sports

  • Switzerland: Embrace the thrill of skiing and snowboarding in the pristine Swiss Alps. Head to St. Moritz, Zermatt, or Davos, renowned for their world-class ski resorts, breathtaking scenery, and luxurious après-ski experiences. Soak in the stunning views of snow-capped mountains, glide down perfectly groomed slopes, and enjoy cozy evenings in charming chalets.

  • Italy: Experience the beauty of the Italian Alps while indulging in your passion for winter sports. Cortina d’Ampezzo, Madonna di Campiglio, and Val Gardena are just a few of the picturesque resorts that offer exhilarating skiing, snowboarding, and cross-country skiing opportunities. Take a break from the slopes to indulge in Italian culinary delights and explore the charming mountain villages.

  • Austria: In addition to its festive charm, Austria also boasts stunning ski resorts in the Tirol region. Kitzbühel, Sölden, and Ischgl are renowned for their challenging slopes, lively après-ski scenes, and breathtaking panoramas. Experience the thrill of skiing amidst the majestic Austrian Alps and enjoy the warm hospitality of the local communities.

Best Countries in Europe for Unique Cultural Experiences

  • Finland: Discover the enchanting Lapland region, home to Santa Claus Village, reindeer sleigh rides, and the chance to witness the Northern Lights. Embrace the Finnish tradition of sauna and take a dip in an icy lake for an invigorating experience. Indulge in traditional Finnish delicacies like glögi (mulled wine) and gingerbread cookies while immersing yourself in the magic of Lapland.

  • Iceland: Immerse yourself in the natural wonders of Iceland during the holiday season. Explore the mesmerizing ice caves, witness the stunning Northern Lights, and take a dip in the geothermal Blue Lagoon. Visit the charming capital city of Reykjavik, adorned with festive decorations, and enjoy unique Icelandic traditions like the bonfires of Jólabrennsla and the feast of Þorrablót.

  • Sweden: Experience the traditional Swedish Christmas celebration known as Lucia, where young girls dressed in white robes and crowns of candles parade through the streets, singing carols. Visit the historic city of Stockholm, where the Christmas markets offer a delightful array of handmade gifts, decorations, and traditional Swedish treats. Take a stroll through the Gamla Stan (Old Town) and be captivated by the charm of cobblestone streets and festive decorations.

No matter your preferences, Europe offers an abundance of incredible destinations to explore in December. Practical information and travel tips#

Settle in for a magical European Christmas adventure. As you plan your trip, keep these handy tips in mind:

  • Dress warmly. December in Europe can be chilly, so pack plenty of warm clothes, including a coat, gloves, and a hat.
  • Book your accommodation early. December is a popular time to visit Europe, so book your accommodation well in advance to avoid disappointment.
  • Consider taking a tour. If you want to make the most of your trip, consider taking a tour. Tours can help you see more in a shorter amount of time and take the hassle out of planning your itinerary.
  • Be prepared for crowds. December is a busy time in Europe, so be prepared for crowds. If you’re visiting popular tourist destinations, be prepared to wait in lines.
  • Enjoy the festive atmosphere. Christmas is a special time in Europe, so take some time to enjoy the festive atmosphere. Visit Christmas markets, go ice skating, or just relax and soak up the holiday spirit.

Top countries in Europe for a December visit

The top countries in Europe for a December visit offer a mesmerizing blend of festive charm, enchanting winter landscapes, and unforgettable experiences. From bustling Christmas markets to snow-capped mountains and captivating cultural traditions, these destinations promise a truly magical holiday season.

Germany: Christmas Market Capital#

Germany, renowned for its Christmas markets, transforms into a winter wonderland in December. Stroll through the festive stalls of Nuremberg’s Christkindlesmarkt, the oldest in the country, or marvel at the grand illuminations of Berlin’s Gendarmenmarkt. Indulge in mulled wine, gingerbread cookies, and traditional handicrafts while soaking up the joyous atmosphere.

Austria: Sound of Music and Snow-capped Mountains#

Austria’s picturesque villages and snow-kissed mountains provide a breathtaking backdrop for a December getaway. Visit Salzburg, the birthplace of Mozart, and immerse yourself in the city’s musical heritage. Explore the charming town of Hallstatt, nestled amidst the Dachstein mountains, or take a sleigh ride through the scenic landscapes of Tyrol.

Finland: Arctic Adventures and Santa’s Home#

Finland offers a unique Arctic experience during the month of December. Head to Lapland, the home of Santa Claus, and embark on a thrilling reindeer or husky safari. Admire the Northern Lights dancing across the sky, or visit the enchanting ice hotels and indulge in traditional Finnish delicacies like glögi and gingerbread cookies.

Norway: Fjords, Snow Sports, and Cozy Hyttes#

Norway’s stunning fjords, snow-covered mountains, and cozy hyttes (cabins) create a picture-perfect winter wonderland. Explore the picturesque town of Bergen, surrounded by majestic mountains and fjords. Enjoy exhilarating winter sports like skiing, snowboarding, and dog sledding in the breathtaking landscapes of the Arctic Circle.

Iceland: Land of Ice and Fire#

Iceland, with its awe-inspiring natural wonders, offers a truly unique December experience. Witness the mesmerizing Northern Lights illuminating the sky, or take a dip in the warm waters of the Blue Lagoon geothermal spa. Explore the dramatic landscapes of Thingvellir National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and marvel at the cascading waterfalls of Gullfoss.

France: Christmas Markets and Festive Illuminations#

France, known for its romantic charm, transforms into a festive wonderland during December. Visit the enchanting Christmas markets of Strasbourg, set against the backdrop of the majestic cathedral, or admire the spectacular illuminations along the Champs-Élysées in Paris. Indulge in delicious French pastries, sip on mulled wine, and soak up the vibrant holiday atmosphere.

United Kingdom: Winter Festivals and Royal Traditions#

England offers a delightful mix of festive traditions and winter celebrations in December. Experience the magic of London’s Winter Wonderland, with its ice rink, festive markets, and dazzling light displays. Admire the elegant Christmas decorations of Oxford and Cambridge or head to Edinburgh for its Hogmanay celebrations, a lively New Year’s Eve festival.

FAQ#

Q1: Which European countries offer the most festive and enchanting experiences during December?#

A1: Among the best countries in Europe for December festivities are Germany, France, Italy, Austria, and Switzerland. These destinations are renowned for their charming Christmas markets, festive decorations, and unique cultural traditions that create a magical atmosphere during the holiday season.

Q2: What are some must-visit Christmas markets in Europe?#

A2: Some of the most popular and enchanting Christmas markets in Europe include the Christkindlmarkt in Nuremberg, Germany; the Strasbourg Christmas Market in France; the Striezelmarkt in Dresden, Germany; the Vienna Christmas Market in Austria; and the Mercato di Natale in Milan, Italy. These markets offer a delightful blend of traditional crafts, local delicacies, and festive entertainment.

Q3: Can you recommend some charming European cities to visit in December for a festive getaway?#

A3: For a truly magical December getaway, consider visiting cities such as Prague, Czech Republic; Budapest, Hungary; Bruges, Belgium; Edinburgh, Scotland; and Copenhagen, Denmark. These cities adorn themselves with festive decorations, offer cozy accommodations, and host a variety of holiday events and attractions.

Q4: Are there any unique cultural traditions or events associated with December celebrations in Europe?#

A4: Yes, each European country has its own unique cultural traditions and events associated with December celebrations. For example, in Germany, there is the tradition of the Weihnachtspyramide (Christmas pyramid), a wooden structure decorated with candles and ornaments. In France, the tradition of the bûche de Noël (Yule log cake) is popular, while in Italy, families gather for the Feast of the Seven Fishes on Christmas Eve.

Q5: What are some tips for planning a December trip to Europe to fully experience the festive atmosphere?#

A5: To fully experience the festive atmosphere during your December trip to Europe, consider visiting during the first week of December to witness the buildup to Christmas and enjoy the markets and decorations. Pack warm clothing and comfortable shoes as the weather can be cold. Make reservations for accommodations and transportation in advance, as it’s a popular time for travel. Take advantage of the opportunity to try local holiday dishes and treats.
