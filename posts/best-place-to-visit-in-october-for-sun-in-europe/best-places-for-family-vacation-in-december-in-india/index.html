Best Places for Family Vacation in December in India: Unforgettable Experiences Await#

With the year-end holidays approaching, it’s time to start planning that unforgettable family vacation in December to India. The diverse landscapes, rich culture, and vibrant cities offer something for every family, making it the perfect destination for a memorable trip.

Top Destinations for a December Family Holiday in India#

  1. Goa: Experience the Best of Sun, Sand, and Family Fun
  • Bask in the sun-kissed beaches of North and South Goa.
  • Explore the fascinating marine life through a fun-filled glass-bottom boat ride.
  • Visit the iconic churches and forts that narrate Goa’s rich history.
  1. Himachal Pradesh: Dive into the Magic of Snowy Mountains
  • Witness the mesmerizing snow-clad mountains, making Himachal a winter wonderland.
  • Enjoy thrilling activities like skiing, snowboarding, and snow tubing.
  • Take a scenic ride on the Kalka-Shimla toy train, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  1. Rajasthan: A Royal Adventure Awaits
  • Step back in time as you explore magnificent palaces and forts in cities like Jaipur, Udaipur, and Jodhpur.
  • Experience the vibrant culture through folk performances, traditional music, and delicious local cuisine.
  • Indulge in a memorable camel safari in the golden Thar Desert.
  1. Kerala: Discover the Enchanting Backwaters
  • Glide through the serene backwaters of Alleppey and Kumarakom on a houseboat.
  • Immerse yourself in the beauty of lush tea plantations in Munnar.
  • Enjoy the unique wildlife experience at Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary.
  1. Andaman and Nicobar Islands: Explore the Pristine Paradise
  • Dive into the crystal-clear waters and encounter vibrant marine life.
  • Relax and sunbathe on the pristine beaches.
  • Embark on an exciting glass-bottom boat ride to witness the breathtaking underwater world.

Tips for Planning Your December Family Vacation in India#

  • Book your flights and accommodations in advance: December is a popular time to travel to India, so make sure to book your arrangements well in advance to secure the best deals and availability.

  • Pack accordingly: Consider the weather conditions at your chosen destinations and pack appropriate clothing and essentials.

  • Plan your itinerary wisely: Prioritize family-friendly activities and attractions that cater to the interests of all age groups.

  • Ensure safety and comfort: Carry a first-aid kit, necessary medications, and travel insurance. Consider hiring a tour guide or driver for hassle-free transportation.

  • Embrace the local culture: Engage in cultural activities, try local delicacies, and interact with the locals to create lasting memories.

Rajasthan: Offers a unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty, with iconic landmarks like the Taj Mahal and the Pink City of Jaipur.#

Soak in the Splendor of Rajasthan’s Cultural Treasures:

Embark on a regal odyssey through Rajasthan, where the sands whisper tales of ancient dynasties and vibrant cultures unfold before your very eyes. Marvel at the architectural brilliance of the Taj Mahal, a sanctuary of love that shimmers in ivory splendor. Immerse yourself in the pulsating heart of Jaipur, the Pink City, where every nook and cranny echoes with a symphony of sounds and colors. Discover the mystique of Mehrangarh Fort, where the echoes of Rajput valor reverberate through the ramparts.

Embrace the Harmony of Nature and Adventure:

Feel the pulse of adventure as you embark on safari expeditions in Ranthambore National Park, where the majestic tiger roams free under the watchful gaze of the Aravali Hills. Gaze upon the shimmering waters of Lake Pichola, where the City Palace of Udaipur floats like a mirage, reflecting the grandeur of a bygone era. Hike through the rugged terrain of the Aravali Range, where nature’s artistry paints a canvas of captivating vistas. Cycle amidst the rustic charm of rural villages, savoring the true essence of Rajasthan’s countryside. Uncover the Enigmatic Beauty of Rajasthan’s Artistic Heritage:

Indulge in the vibrant artistry of Rajasthan, where each brushstroke tells a tale. Discover the intricate designs and lively hues of Rajasthani miniature paintings, a testament to the region’s rich artistic legacy. Shop for exquisite handicrafts in Jaipur’s bustling bazaars, where the air hums with the rhythm of bargaining. Lose yourself in the captivating performances of Rajasthani folk dances, where swirling skirts and joyous beats weave a magical tapestry of tradition.

Key Takeaways:

  • Immerse yourself in Rajasthan’s cultural tapestry, exploring iconic landmarks like the Taj Mahal, Jaipur, and Mehrangarh Fort.
  • Embark on thrilling adventures in Ranthambore National Park, Lake Pichola, and the Aravali Range.
  • Discover Rajasthan’s artistic heritage through miniature paintings, handicrafts, and folk performances.
  • Experience the warmth of Rajasthani hospitality, making memories that will last a lifetime.

Kerala: Known for its scenic backwaters, lush greenery, and diverse wildlife, providing a serene and tranquil vacation experience.#

Welcome to God’s Own Country, where nature’s beauty and tranquility blend harmoniously to create a perfect sanctuary for families seeking a memorable December vacation!

Kerala’s Enchanting Charms:

  • Backwaters: Picture serene waterways meandering through palm-fringed landscapes, offering a tranquil escape aboard traditional houseboats.

  • Lush Greenery: Verdant hills, tropical rainforests, and spice plantations provide a breathtaking backdrop for your family adventures.

  • Wildlife: Encounter the wonders of nature at Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, home to elephants, tigers, and a diverse array of birdlife.

  • Beaches: Miles of golden sands and gentle waves create the perfect setting for sun-kissed days and unforgettable moments on the shore.

  • Ayurveda: Immerse yourself in the ancient healing traditions of Ayurveda, rejuvenating your mind, body, and soul.

  • Culture: Delve into the rich cultural heritage of Kerala, exploring ancient temples, historic sites, and vibrant local traditions.

Must-Visit Destinations for Families:

  • Alleppey: Embark on a serene houseboat journey through the backwaters, experiencing the tranquil beauty of Kerala’s waterways.

  • Munnar: Discover rolling tea plantations, cascading waterfalls, and picturesque viewpoints amidst the enchanting hills of Munnar.

  • Thekkady: Explore the lush greenery of Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, where wildlife encounters and stunning landscapes await.

  • Kovalam: Indulge in sun-filled days and water sports along the idyllic beaches of Kovalam.

  • Fort Kochi: Journey through history and culture in Fort Kochi, where colonial architecture, art galleries, and traditional markets captivate visitors.

  • Wayanad: Nestled amidst the Western Ghats, Wayanad offers breathtaking vistas, dense forests, and opportunities for thrilling outdoor adventures.

Key Takeaways:

  • Kerala: An ideal destination for families seeking a serene and enriching vacation in December.
  • Backwaters, lush greenery, diverse wildlife, and pristine beaches: Create a harmonious blend of nature’s beauty and tranquility.
  • Must-visit destinations: Alleppey, Munnar, Thekkady, Kovalam, Fort Kochi, and Wayanad offer a range of experiences for families.
  • Activities: Houseboat cruises, wildlife safaris, plantation visits, beach days, and cultural exploration provide unforgettable moments.
  • Ayurveda: Rejuvenate and revitalize with traditional healing practices, restoring balance and well-being.

Uttarakhand: Offers stunning mountain views, picturesque hill stations, and opportunities for adventure activities like trekking and camping.#

Majestic mountains, scenic hill stations, and exciting adventure activities await families in Uttarakhand, a state nestled in the Himalayas. From serene lakes to gushing rivers, ancient temples to vibrant culture, Uttarakhand promises a memorable family vacation.

Thrilling Adventure Activities:#

  • Trek through the scenic Valley of Flowers National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and witness a mesmerizing tapestry of alpine flora.

  • Embark on a camping expedition in the Jim Corbett National Park, renowned for its diverse wildlife, including the majestic Bengal tiger.

  • Take a refreshing dip in the natural hot springs of Joshimath, believed to possess therapeutic properties.

  • Uncover the spiritual side of Uttarakhand with a visit to the holy cities of Haridwar and Rishikesh.

Places to Visit:#

  1. Nainital: A charming hill station with a beautiful lake, Nainital is perfect for boating and leisurely walks.

  2. Mussoorie: Known as the Queen of the Hills, Mussoorie offers stunning views of the Himalayas and is famous for its colonial architecture.

  3. Rishikesh: A pilgrimage center and yoga hub, Rishikesh is also an excellent starting point for white-water rafting and bungee jumping.

  4. Auli: A popular ski destination, Auli offers breathtaking views of the Nanda Devi peak, the second-highest mountain in India.

Key Takeaways:

  • Uttarakhand offers a diverse range of experiences for families, from adventure activities to spiritual journeys.

  • The state is home to some of the most stunning mountain views and picturesque hill stations in India.

  • Uttarakhand is an ideal destination for families seeking an active and memorable vacation.

  • The state offers a variety of accommodations, from budget-friendly guesthouses to luxurious resorts.

  • Uttarakhand is well-connected by air, rail, and road, making it easily accessible from major cities in India.

FAQ#

Q1: Which destinations in India offer the best family-friendly experiences in December?#

A1: Some of the best family-friendly destinations in India for a December vacation include Shimla, Goa, Kerala, Rajasthan, and Andaman and Nicobar Islands, each offering unique attractions, activities, and cultural experiences suitable for families with children of different ages.

Q2: What are some of the key factors to consider when planning a family vacation in India during December?#

A2: When planning a family vacation in India during December, it’s important to consider factors such as weather conditions, availability of family-friendly activities and attractions, safety, and overall value for money. Additionally, it’s crucial to research and book accommodations, transportation, and tours in advance to ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience.

Q3: Are there any specific activities or attractions that are particularly suitable for families with young children in December?#

A3: Yes, there are numerous activities and attractions in India that cater specifically to families with young children. These may include visits to amusement parks, wildlife sanctuaries, interactive museums, cultural shows, and kid-friendly beaches. Many destinations also offer opportunities for outdoor activities like hiking, cycling, and boat rides that can be tailored to suit the interests and abilities of younger family members.

Q4: What are some tips for ensuring a safe and enjoyable family vacation in India during December?#

A4: To ensure a safe and enjoyable family vacation in India during December, it’s important to plan your itinerary carefully, considering factors like weather conditions and the suitability of activities for your family members’ ages and interests. Additionally, it’s crucial to pack essential items such as appropriate clothing, medications, and travel documents. It’s also advisable to research local customs and traditions and dress and behave respectfully to avoid causing offense.

Q5: How can I find the best deals on accommodations, transportation, and tours for a family vacation in India in December?#

A5: To find the best deals on accommodations, transportation, and tours for a family vacation in India in December, it’s recommended to book early to take advantage of discounts and promotions. Additionally, consider traveling during the shoulder season (November-December) to avoid the peak tourist season and potentially save money. It’s also helpful to research and compare prices from different providers and consider packages that offer bundled services at a reduced cost.
