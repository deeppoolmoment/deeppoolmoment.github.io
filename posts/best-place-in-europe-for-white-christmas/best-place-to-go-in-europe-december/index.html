Best Place to Go in Europe December#

In December, Europe transforms into a winter wonderland, adorned with twinkling lights, festive markets, and snow-capped mountains. From charming villages to bustling cities, each destination offers unique experiences to embrace the magic of the season.

1. Lapland, Finland:#

  • Experience the Magic of the Arctic Circle: Discover the enchanting Lapland region, nestled within the Arctic Circle. Witness the ethereal beauty of the Northern Lights, embark on thrilling husky safaris, and meet the friendly reindeer.

  • Santa’s Home: Visit the iconic Santa Claus Village in Rovaniemi, where you can meet Santa himself, send postcards from the Arctic Circle, and indulge in festive activities.

2. Prague, Czech Republic:#

  • Stroll Through a Fairytale City: Step into the pages of a storybook as you wander through Prague’s enchanting Old Town Square. Admire the astronomical clock, marvel at the Gothic architecture, and sip mulled wine at the bustling Christmas markets.

  • Explore Hidden Gems: Venture beyond the main attractions to uncover Prague’s hidden gems, like the charming John Lennon Wall, the serene Kampa Island, and the majestic Vyšehrad Castle.

3. Budapest, Hungary:#

  • Thermal Baths and Festive Delights: Immerse yourself in the warmth of Budapest’s renowned thermal baths, surrounded by stunning architecture. Explore the city’s vibrant Christmas markets, offering traditional Hungarian delicacies and handcrafted souvenirs.

  • Discover Budapest’s Charm: Take a romantic stroll along the Danube River, admire the grandeur of the Hungarian Parliament Building, and enjoy a traditional Hungarian dinner in a cozy restaurant.

4. Vienna, Austria:#

  • Christmas Markets and Imperial Elegance: Experience the grandeur of Vienna during the holiday season, with its elegant Christmas markets set against the backdrop of imperial palaces. Indulge in Viennese delicacies, listen to classical concerts, and soak in the festive atmosphere.

  • Explore Artistic Treasures: Immerse yourself in Vienna’s rich artistic heritage. Visit the Kunsthistorisches Museum, admire the works of Klimt and Schiele at the Belvedere Museum, and attend a performance at the Vienna State Opera.

5. Bruges, Belgium:#

  • A Medieval Fairytale: Step back in time as you wander through the cobblestone streets of Bruges, lined with charming medieval buildings. Visit the Markt, where the Christmas market fills the air with festive aromas, and take a romantic boat ride along the canals.

  • Taste Belgian Delights: Indulge in Belgium’s renowned chocolates, waffles, and beer. Visit the Choco-Story Museum, sample local beers at a traditional pub, and enjoy a hearty meal at a cozy Flemish restaurant.

6. Copenhagen, Denmark:#

  • Hygge and Scandinavian Charm: Experience the cozy Danish concept of “hygge” in Copenhagen. Stroll through the Tivoli Gardens, transformed into a winter wonderland with twinkling lights and festive decorations. Enjoy a traditional Danish Christmas dinner and sip on gløgg, a warm mulled wine.

  • Explore Design and Architecture: Discover Copenhagen’s modern design and architecture, from the iconic Royal Danish Opera House to the colorful Nyhavn district. Visit the Designmuseum Danmark to learn about Danish design history and admire the works of iconic designers.

7. Amsterdam, Netherlands:#

  • Canals, Lights, and Festive Cheer: Embrace the festive spirit as you glide along Amsterdam’s picturesque canals, adorned with twinkling lights and decorations. Visit the Christmas markets, indulge in Dutch delicacies like oliebollen and poffertjes, and enjoy the lively atmosphere of the city.

  • Explore Artistic Treasures: Immerse yourself in Amsterdam’s rich art scene. Visit the Rijksmuseum to admire Rembrandt’s masterpieces, explore the Van Gogh Museum, and discover the works of contemporary artists at the Stedelijk Museum.

8. Nuremberg, Germany:#

  • A Traditional Christmas Experience: Experience the magic of a traditional German Christmas in Nuremberg, home to one of the oldest and most famous Christmas markets in the world. Admire the intricate decorations, sample gingerbread and mulled wine, and browse the stalls for unique gifts.

  • Explore Historical Treasures: Delve into Nuremberg’s rich history and culture. Visit the Imperial Castle, learn about the city’s past at the German National Museum, and walk along the medieval city walls for stunning views.

Experience the Festive Ambiance of European Christmas Markets#

Prepare to immerse yourself in the breathtaking magic of Europe’s most enchanting Christmas markets, where festive cheer fills the air and the spirit of the season comes alive. From the snow-kissed streets of Germany to the twinkling alleyways of France, discover the best places to revel in the joyous traditions and warm hospitality that define Europe’s winter wonderland.

Germany: A Christmas Market Paradise#

  • Nuremberg: Step into a fairytale realm at the Nuremberg Christkindlesmarkt, Germany’s most renowned Christmas market. Admire the intricate decorations, savor the mouthwatering gingerbread and mulled wine, and let the enchanting atmosphere transport you to a world of pure magic.

  • Dresden: Wander through the bustling stalls of the Striezelmarkt, Germany’s oldest Christmas market. Experience the charm of traditional crafts, indulge in delectable treats like Dresdner Christstollen, and witness the awe-inspiring pageant of the Three Wise Men.

  • Munich: Immerse yourself in the merriments of the Munich Christmas market, held in the heart of the city’s historic center. Sip on steaming mugs of Glühwein, browse through an array of handcrafted gifts, and be mesmerized by the spectacular light displays that illuminate the night sky.

France: Enchanting Christmas Markets#

  • Strasbourg: Embark on a journey through the enchanting Christmas markets of Strasbourg, where fairy lights twinkle and festive decorations adorn every corner. Admire the grandeur of the Strasbourg Cathedral, indulge in Alsatian delicacies like bredele and foie gras, and soak in the vibrant atmosphere that makes this city a true winter wonderland.

  • Colmar: Step back in time as you explore the picturesque Christmas markets of Colmar, a charming town nestled in the heart of the Alsace region. Discover hidden gems tucked away in cobblestone streets, savor the flavors of traditional Alsatian cuisine, and lose yourself in the captivating beauty of this festive wonderland.

  • Paris: Experience the magic of Christmas in the City of Lights, where the Champs-Élysées transforms into a glittering promenade of lights and the Eiffel Tower sparkles against the winter sky. Browse through the stalls of the Christmas market at the Place Vendôme, warm up with a cup of vin chaud, and let the enchanting ambiance of Paris cast its spell on you.

Other European Christmas Market Gems#

  • Vienna, Austria: Explore the elegant Christmas markets of Vienna, where imperial grandeur meets festive cheer. Admire the stunning architecture of the Rathausplatz, indulge in Viennese delicacies like Sachertorte and Kaiserschmarrn, and be captivated by the melodies of Christmas carols filling the air.

  • Prague, Czech Republic: Immerse yourself in the enchanting atmosphere of Prague’s Christmas markets, set against the backdrop of the city’s historic center. Marvel at the Astronomical Clock, sip on svařák (mulled wine), and savor traditional Czech treats like vánoční cukroví (Christmas cookies) as you soak in the festive spirit of this charming city.

  • Bruges, Belgium: Wander through the picturesque streets of Bruges, adorned with twinkling lights and festive decorations. Discover unique gifts at the Christmas market in the Markt, sample Belgian chocolates and waffles, and enjoy the warmth of a crackling fire in one of the city’s cozy cafes.

Key Takeaways:

  • Germany, France, Austria, the Czech Republic, and Belgium offer some of the most enchanting Christmas markets in Europe.

  • Each market has its own unique charm and traditions, from traditional crafts and delectable treats to festive decorations and enchanting light displays.

  • Whether you’re seeking the grandeur of a city like Paris or the charm of a quaint town like Colmar, Europe’s Christmas markets offer an unforgettable winter experience that will leave you filled with joy and wonder.

Explore historic cities adorned with holiday decorations#

Deck the halls and immerse yourself in the festive charm of Europe’s historic cities this December. From sparkling lights illuminating cobblestone streets to enchanting Christmas markets brimming with local delicacies and handcrafted gifts, these destinations will surely ignite your holiday spirit.

Prague, Czech Republic:#

Wander through the picturesque streets of Prague’s Old Town, where towering spires and Gothic architecture provide a backdrop to the city’s festive spirit. Admire the twinkling lights along Charles Bridge and soak in the sights and sounds of the bustling Christmas markets in Old Town Square.

Budapest, Hungary:#

Stroll along the banks of the Danube River and marvel at the city’s iconic landmarks adorned with holiday lights. Visit the enchanting Christmas markets at Vörösmarty Square, where you can indulge in traditional Hungarian treats and browse unique handmade gifts.

Vienna, Austria:#

Experience the imperial grandeur of Vienna during the holiday season. Explore the magnificent Schönbrunn Palace, where Christmas decorations transform the gardens into a winter wonderland. Take a leisurely walk through the city center and admire the festive illuminations lining the streets.

Bruges, Belgium:#

Step into a storybook setting as you explore the medieval city of Bruges. Horse-drawn carriages clip-clop through cobblestone streets, while Christmas markets offer an array of festive treats and local crafts. Don’t miss the awe-inspiring light projections on the city’s historic buildings.

Copenhagen, Denmark:#

Embrace the hygge lifestyle in Copenhagen, where cozy Christmas markets and twinkling lights create a warm and inviting atmosphere. Take a stroll along the picturesque Nyhavn waterfront and admire the colorful buildings adorned with festive decorations.

Key Takeaways:#

  • Explore historic cities adorned with holiday decorations across Europe, from Prague’s enchanting Old Town to Budapest’s bustling Christmas markets.
  • Stroll through the festive streets of Vienna, admire the grandeur of Schönbrunn Palace, and immerse yourself in Bruges’ medieval charm.
  • Experience the Danish hygge lifestyle in Copenhagen, with cozy Christmas markets and twinkling lights along the Nyhavn waterfront.
  • Immerse yourself in the festive spirit of Europe’s historic cities, where the magic of the holiday season comes alive.

Discover enchanting winter landscapes and natural wonders#

Ever dreamt of stepping into a winter wonderland in Europe? Picture snow-covered peaks, frosty forests, and frozen lakes against the backdrop of quaint villages and magnificent castles. December transforms Europe into a realm of enchantment, offering a breathtaking spectacle of nature’s artistry. Join me on a captivating journey to explore the most enchanting winter destinations in Europe, where the magic of the season awaits.

Enchanted Winter Destinations in Europe#

1. Salzburg, Austria:

Stroll through the snow-covered streets of Salzburg, the birthplace of Mozart, and immerse yourself in its festive charm. Marvel at the majestic Hohensalzburg Fortress, glistening with snow, and take a horse-drawn carriage ride through the Old Town. Don’t miss the enchanting Christmas markets, where you can indulge in festive treats and find unique gifts.

2. Lapland, Finland:

Embark on a once-in-a-lifetime adventure in Lapland, the home of Santa Claus. Experience the thrill of reindeer sleigh rides, snowmobiling across frozen lakes, and dog sledding through snowy forests. Stay in a cozy log cabin, witness the surreal beauty of the Northern Lights, and make unforgettable memories in this winter wonderland.

3. Iceland:

Iceland offers a mesmerizing blend of snow-capped mountains, glaciers, and geothermal wonders. Take a dip in the warm waters of the Blue Lagoon, surrounded by a snowy landscape. Explore the awe-inspiring Jökulsárlón Glacier Lagoon, where you can witness icebergs floating amidst crystal-clear waters. Don’t miss the opportunity to see the magnificent Northern Lights dancing across the sky.

4. Paris, France:

Paris transforms into a winter wonderland, with its iconic landmarks adorned with festive lights. Stroll along the Champs-Élysées, marvel at the twinkling Eiffel Tower, and take in the breathtaking views from the Sacré-Cœur Basilica. Enjoy a warm cup of café au lait in a cozy café, and experience the magic of Paris during the holiday season.

5. Prague, Czech Republic:

Prague’s Old Town Square is a sight to behold during winter. Admire the Astronomical Clock, with its intricate details, and explore the charming Christmas markets. Take a walk across the Charles Bridge, marveling at the snow-covered cityscape, and visit Prague Castle for panoramic views of the city.

6. Bruges, Belgium:

Step into a medieval fairytale in Bruges, where canals are adorned with twinkling lights and snow-covered bridges create a picturesque scene. Take a boat tour along the canals, visit the Markt Square with its stunning architecture, and indulge in the delicious Belgian chocolates and waffles.

Key Takeaways:

  • Europe offers a plethora of enchanting winter destinations that showcase the beauty of the season.
  • From snowy landscapes and frozen lakes to cozy villages and festive markets, these destinations offer a unique blend of natural wonders and cultural experiences.
  • Whether you seek adventure, relaxation, or a romantic getaway, Europe’s winter destinations have something for everyone.
  • Embrace the magic of the season and create unforgettable memories in the enchanting winter landscapes of Europe.
